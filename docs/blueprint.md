# **App Name**: Central da Visão - Excellence in Sight

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Display a clear, concise hero section with key information and a prominent call to action to 'Schedule a Consultation.'
- Doctor Profile: Showcase Dr. Caio Renato Alcântara Mota's profile with his credentials, a brief bio, and a CTA to view available exams.
- Services and Exams Display: List the services and exams offered in a visually appealing card format with icons for each service.  Includes CTAs to 'Schedule an Exam'.
- Differentiators Section: Highlight the clinic's key differentiators with icons or a bulleted list and include a call to action to 'Contact the Clinic.'
- Location Integration: Embed a Google Maps integration displaying the clinic's location, address, and hours, with a fixed button to 'Open in Google Maps'.
- Quick Contact Section: Implement a contact section with quick access to the clinic's email, WhatsApp, and a simple form for quick inquiries with a centralized button 'Send consultation request'.
- Whatsapp integration: Implement a WhatsApp floating button to easily schedule a direct consultation

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#673DE6) for a sense of sophistication and specialized care.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F0F0) to provide a clean and subtle backdrop that ensures the focus remains on the content and avoids distracting the user.
- Accent color: A softer violet (#9475EB) to complement the primary purple and maintain a consistent visual experience.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern and accessible feel. This font will be used throughout the site for headings and body text due to its versatility.
- Use a set of modern, minimalist icons in the primary purple (#673DE6) to represent services, differentiators, and contact methods.
- Design a fully responsive layout that adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes (desktop, tablet, mobile) ensuring an optimal viewing experience across all devices.
- Incorporate subtle hover animations on buttons and interactive elements to provide visual feedback and enhance user engagement.
